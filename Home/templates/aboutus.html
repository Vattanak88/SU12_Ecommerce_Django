Today, we proudly serve over 15,000 businesses across all 25 provinces of Cambodia and have expanded our reach to ASEAN neighbors.

Our Journey

From a garage in Toul Kork to Cambodia's #1 communication platform

2018 - The Beginning

Founded by three friends in a small office in Phnom Penh with just $5,000 and a dream to make business communication easier in Cambodia.

2019 - First 1,000 Customers

Reached 1,000 businesses within 12 months. Launched Khmer-language interface and Wing/ABA integration.

2021 - National Expansion

Opened offices in Siem Reap, Battambang, and Sihanoukville. Became the first Cambodian company to offer Telegram Business API.

2023 - ASEAN Launch

Expanded to Vietnam and Laos. Raised $2M from regional investors. Now serving 10,000+ businesses.

2025 - Today

Cambodia's most trusted communication platform with 15,000+ customers and growing 150% year-over-year.
